

# Health Benefits of Skateboarding

## Physical Health

- Regular physical activity helps improve cardiovascular fitness and supports heart health when paired with other healthy habits.
- Skateboarding engages multiple muscle groups, improving balance, coordination, and endurance.
- Staying active can support a healthy body weight, which is one factor that lowers heart disease risk.

## Mental Health and Stress Relief

- Many skaters ride for fun and stress relief, which can support better overall mental health.
- Focused riding can create a sense of flow that reduces anxiety and overthinking.
- Learning tricks and persisting after falls can build confidence and coping skills.

## Community and Wellness

- Skateparks often foster inclusive communities where people connect across different backgrounds and ages.
- Acts of kindness and informal coaching at skateparks create supportive social networks.
- Feeling accepted in a skate community can provide a strong sense of belonging.
